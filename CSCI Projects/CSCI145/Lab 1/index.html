The Chicago Bulls are an NBA basketball team based in Chicago, Illinois who play in the United Center.
The team was founded on January 16, 1966.
The Chicago Bulls currently have six championships in the years 1991, 1992, 1993, 1996, 1997, and 1998.
The Bulls are the only NBA team to win multiple championships while never losing an NBA Finals series in their history of competing in one.
The Bulls roster is made up of upcoming talents such as PG Kris Dunn, SG Zach Lavine, PF Lauri Markkanen, and PF Nikola Mirotic.
The current owner of the Chicago Bulls is Jerry Reinsdorf, the current GM is Gar Forman, and the current head coach is Fred Hoiberg.
